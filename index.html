    Privacy Policy

    1. Introduction

    ShipInstant operates a logistics and shipping platform available through our website and mobile application (together, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices available to you.

    By creating an account or otherwise using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the Service.

    Privacy Policy

    2. Information We Collect

    Information you provide

    • Account details — your full name, email address, and phone number when you register or update your profile.
    • Shipment details — pickup and delivery addresses, recipient information, and package details you enter to create a shipment.
    • Support communications — anything you share when you contact our support team.

    Information collected automatically

    • Usage data — pages and screens viewed, features used, and actions taken within the Service.
    • Device and log data — device type, operating system, app version, IP address, and approximate location derived from your IP address.
    • Cookies on our website, used to keep you signed in and understand how the site is used. See "Cookies" below.

    Payment information

    We do not collect or store your payment card, bank, or PayPal account credentials. Payments are handled entirely by PayPal — see Section 4 below.

    Privacy Policy

    3. How We Use Your Information

    • To create and manage your account
    • To process and track shipments, and keep you updated on their status
    • To process payments through PayPal and send order confirmations or receipts
    • To respond to support requests
    • To send service-related notifications and, only with your consent, marketing communications
    • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
    • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions
    • To maintain and improve the reliability of the Service

    Privacy Policy

    4. Payment Processing — PayPal

    All payments made through ShipInstant are processed by PayPal on the web. When you choose to pay, you're redirected to PayPal to complete the transaction securely on PayPal's own platform.

    ShipInstant does not collect, transmit, or store your card number, bank account details, or PayPal login credentials — no payment data is saved within the ShipInstant app or on our servers. We may receive limited transaction confirmation data from PayPal, such as a transaction ID, payment status, and amount, which we use to confirm your order and maintain your shipment records.

    Your relationship with PayPal regarding payment processing is governed separately by PayPal's own privacy policy and user agreement.

    Privacy Policy

    5. How We Share Your Information

    We share information only as needed to operate the Service:

    • PayPal, to process payments
    • Couriers, carriers, and delivery partners, to fulfil and track your shipments
    • Service providers who support hosting, analytics, customer support, and notifications, under confidentiality obligations
    • Authorities or other third parties, where required by law, to enforce our Terms, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of ShipInstant, our users, or the public
    • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets — we'll notify you if your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy

    We do not sell your personal information.

    Privacy Policy

    6. Data Storage & Security

    We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, and regular review of our systems. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

    Privacy Policy

    7. Data Retention

    We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account at any time, as described in Section 8.

    Privacy Policy

    8. Your Rights & Choices

    Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

    • Access the personal information we hold about you
    • Correct inaccurate information
    • Request deletion of your account and associated data
    • Object to or restrict certain processing
    • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

    To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 13. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

    Privacy Policy

    9. Cookies

    Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some features of the website.

    Privacy Policy

    10. Children's Privacy

    The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

    Privacy Policy

    11. International Data Transfers

    Your information may be processed in countries other than the one you live in. Where required, we put appropriate safeguards in place for these transfers in line with applicable data protection law.

    Privacy Policy

    12. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the "Effective" date shown at the top of this page and, for material changes, provide additional notice — such as an in-app notification or email.
